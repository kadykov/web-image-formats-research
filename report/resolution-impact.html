Resolution Impact Study

Study how input resolution affects compression efficiency across different image formats (JPEG, WebP, AVIF, JPEG XL). Tests the same images at multiple downscaled resolutions to understand resolution-dependent performance characteristics. Quality settings are chosen to target around 80 SSIMULACRA2 score for each format at the original resolution.

Dataset: div2k-valid (12 images) · 432 measurements · Formats: AVIF, JPEG, JXL, WEBP
